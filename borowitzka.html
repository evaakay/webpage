Borowitzka


Work for a dear friend, young cinematographer, director Matej Pinos.
Matej came to me to help him design a visual identity for Borowitzka, a pseudonym he used in his early cinematographic career. Borowitzka means a pine tree which is also a play on Matej's last name.
