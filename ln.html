Branding

The logo design process for attorney LN started with a short conversation regarding the desired style, feel, and colors of the logo. The logo comprises the LN inicials typed in a serif font that is formal yet simple and elegant. The letters are blended together and further simplified but remain legible. The graphic element of a red dot anchors the logo on a page and subtly references the logo when used elsewhere on a page, e.g. as bullet points.
