Hi! It is nice to meet you!

My name is Eva Kodouskova /Eva Kay for short/ and I was that kid that was always drawing.

For as long as I remember, my friends would ask me for a help with various design projects. Over the time I've created sweet designs I'm truly proud of and decided to share some of them with the world. I love the creative process and designing something people love and use.

When I travel I always bring my sketchbook. I love to visit new places and sketch the architecture while having a glass of wine.

As for my profession, I am an architect in Washington, DC and just like many of my peers I've chosen architecture for the lovely balance of creativity and technical understanding.

I hope this page brings you joy and inspiration.
Yours truly,
