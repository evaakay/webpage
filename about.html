HELLO!





My name is EVA and I'm so thrilled to meet you!

I love to travel with my camera to places where I've never been, admire architecture and enjoy local food (and wine). Our world is full of beautiful places and I would love to see as much of it as possible. I love the moments that take my breath away. I am hungry for knowledge and experiences. I always immerse myself in my work until it's all done – which turns out to be equally good and bad thing. I suffer from severe "fear of missing out" and so I always try to squeeze the maximum into each day of my life.

As for my profession, I am an architect in Washington, DC and just like many of my peers I've chosen architecture for the lovely balance of creativity and technical understanding. I've always wanted to create and design as well as to figure out how stuff works!

Since I was little, I've loved to draw. Drawing and sketching can keep me busy for hours. When my friends needed aesthetic opinion or a new logo they would often ask for my help. So somewhat naturally, I found myself working on projects for my friends, honing my graphic design skills and enjoying every second of it. If I am not designing you will find me outdoors, probably exploring some mountains or racing triathlons.

Now, let's hear about you and what you are up to! I would love to learn about your ideas and hopefully collaborate with you to create something awesome! Drop me a line!
