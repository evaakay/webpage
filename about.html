hi -

My name is Eva Kodouskova /Eva Kay for short/ and it is nice to meet you!

As long as I remember, I was the kid who was always drawing, creating and building. I loved that stuff so much I went to architecture school. Over time, my friends would ask me for a help with various design projects and I had the opprotunity to create sweet designs I'm truly proud of. I've decided to share some of them with the world (you). The creative process is such an adventure!

Now, as an architect, I carry my sketchbook everywhere. Sketching allows me to experience and understand a building. I love to travel to new places and sketch the architecture while sipping some wine.

I hope this page brings you joy and inspiration.
Yours truly,

-eva
