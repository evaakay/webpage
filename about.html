My name is EVA and I'm so thrilled to meet you!

Since I was little, I've loved to draw. Drawing and sketching can keep me busy for hours. When I travel I always bring my sketchbook. I love to visit places where I've never been, sketch local architecture and enjoy local food (and wine).
I often found myself helping my friends with creating logos, brands, tshirt designs and eventually desigining apps and websites. I really enjoy being part of the creative process and creating something people identify with and love. When I am not woring on any design projects, I am probably traveling or spending my time outdoors. Lately I've been obsessed by racing triathlons and backpacking.

As for my profession, I am an architect in Washington, DC and just like many of my peers I've chosen architecture for the lovely balance of creativity and technical understanding. I've always wanted to create and design as well as to figure out how stuff works!

Now let's hear about you! I would love to learn about your projects and help to develop the perfect design for you!
