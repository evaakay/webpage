Za lepší nádech/ For an easier breath


For an easier breath is a fundarising event which raises awareness of cystic fibrosis. For an easier breath is trying to familiarize public with the ilness and describe day to day challenges of CF patients. The event includes a basketball game between the Czech women's national team and Czech celebrities. Famous Czech athletes such as Martina Sablikova, Tomas Satoransky or Simona Baumrtova also donated personal items for auction. Besides raising funds for better treatment of CF, the goal of this event is to raise awarness of cystic fibrosis.
