Za lepší nádech/ For an easier breath

Za lepsi nadech, translated as "For an easier breath", is a fundarising event in Prague, Czech Republic that raises awareness of cystic fibrosis. The main part of the program is a basketball game between the Czech women's national team and a team assembled of Czech celebrities. To learn more about the initiatives and ways to donate please visit Za lepsi nadech. Photos courtesy of Patrik Nguyen.

The logo is shaped as a set of lungs with a child outline in its negative space. The design is simple, legible and emphasizes the strength of all children suffering with cystic fibrosis.
